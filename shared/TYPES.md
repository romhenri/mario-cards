# TYPE IDENTITY RULES

Abilities that define the identity of each family/species. Every new card
in these lines must follow its rule (and pay the ability's penalty on the
curve — see BALANCED.md).

## RULES

* **Piranha Plants** (Piranha Plant, Venus Fire Trap, Piranha Creeper): always have **Taunt**
* **Pokeys** (Pokey, Snow Pokey): always have **Quick**
* **Bros** (Hammer, Fire, Ice, Boomerang, Sledge): always have **Reach**
* **Boos / ghosts** (Boo, Big Boo, King Boo): always have **Stealth + Fly**
* **Moles** (Monty, Morty, Rocky Wrench): always have **Stealth**
* **Shelled Koopas** (Koopa Troopa, Paratroopa, Dry Bones, Para Dry Bones, Chargin' Chuck): always have **Shield**
* **Bullet Bills** (Bullet Bill, Banzai Bill, Cat Bullet Bill): always have **Bomb**
* **Para-/winged variants** (Paragoomba, Winged Galoomba, Paratroopa, Para Dry Bones, Para-Bomb, Para-Biddybud, Fly Guy...): always have **Fly**
* **Grand variants** (Grand Goomba, Grand Galoomba): always have **Taunt**
* **Chomp family** (Chomp, Bully): always have **Taunt**
* **Yoshis** (Yoshi, Blue, Red, Yellow): cost 5 and always have **Reach**
